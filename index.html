Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

Thank you for using Scrapbook. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

Information We Collect

Depending on how you use Scrapbook, we may collect:

  • Name and email address (when signing in).
  • Profile photo (if provided through Google Sign-In).
  • Photos and scrapbook images you upload.
  • Account preferences and profile information.
  • Device notification token for push notifications.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Store your scrapbook memories securely.
  • Enable sharing features within the app.
  • Send push notifications when enabled.
  • Improve app functionality and reliability.
  • Maintain security and prevent abuse.

Services We Use

Scrapbook uses trusted third-party services including:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Cloud Firestore
  • Firebase Storage
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging

These services may process certain information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Photo Storage

Photos you upload are stored securely in cloud storage so they can be accessed from your account across devices.

Push Notifications

If you enable notifications, your device receives a notification token used solely for delivering notifications. You may disable notifications at any time from your device settings.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information using secure cloud infrastructure and authentication mechanisms. However, no method of electronic storage or internet transmission is completely secure.

Your Rights

You may:

  • Update your account information.
  • Delete your account.
  • Request deletion of your stored data.

Children's Privacy

Scrapbook is not intended for children under 13 years of age.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
