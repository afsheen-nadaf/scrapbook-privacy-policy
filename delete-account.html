Delete Your scrapbook Account

You can permanently delete your Scrapbook account directly within the app.

Steps

  1. Open the Scrapbook app.
  2. Sign in to your account.
  3. Go to your Profile.
  4. Tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm the deletion.

What data is deleted?

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently deleted:

  • Your Firebase Authentication account.
  • Your profile information.
  • Your scrapbook posts and memories.
  • Uploaded photos and images.
  • Associated notification tokens.

Data retention

Once account deletion is confirmed, your account and associated data are permanently deleted from our systems. We do not retain copies of your personal content after deletion, except where retention is required by applicable law.
