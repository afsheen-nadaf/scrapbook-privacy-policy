Scrapbook Child Safety Standards

At Scrapbook, we are committed to providing a safe and respectful environment for all users. We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) or any content that exploits, endangers, or harms children.

Our Commitment

  • We prohibit any content involving child sexual abuse or exploitation.
  • Users must not upload, share, request, or distribute illegal or exploitative content.
  • Accounts found engaging in such activity may be suspended or permanently removed.
  • We cooperate with applicable law enforcement authorities where required by law.

Reporting

Users who encounter content or behavior that violates these standards should contact us immediately so that appropriate action can be taken.
